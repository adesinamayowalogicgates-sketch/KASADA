# KASADA Brand Guidelines
*Version 1.0 | April 2026*

---

## 1. Brand Essence
KASADA is Nigeria's premier destination for handcrafted furniture. We bridge the gap between local artisans and modern homes, creating a cultural bridge through design.

### Mission
To empower Nigerian artisans by providing a global platform for their craftsmanship, while offering homeowners unique, high-quality furniture that tells a story.

### Vision
To become the definitive global destination for Afro-contemporary design, setting the standard for ethical production and artisanal luxury.

### Core Principles
1. **Authenticity**: We celebrate the raw beauty of Nigerian materials and the honesty of hand-carved details.
2. **Modernity**: We bridge traditional motifs with contemporary silhouettes for the global home.
3. **Transparency**: From artisan wages to shipping timelines, we believe in radical honesty.

## 2. Visual Identity

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Onyx** | `#0a0a0a` | Primary text, backgrounds, and deep accents. |
| **Alabaster** | `#fafaf9` | Main background, negative space, and clean surfaces. |
| **Copper** | `#b45309` | Primary accent, call-to-actions, and highlights. |
| **Slate** | `#475569` | Secondary text, metadata, and subtle borders. |

### Typography
- **Primary Display (Headings)**: `Playfair Display` (Serif)
  - *Vibe*: Elegant, traditional, authoritative.
  - *Usage*: Hero sections, page titles, brand statements.
- **Secondary UI (Body)**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` (Sans-Serif)
  - *Vibe*: Modern, clean, highly readable.
  - *Usage*: Body copy, navigation, functional UI elements.

---

## 3. Design Language

### Layout & Spacing
- **Generous White Space**: Emphasize luxury and focus on the product.
- **Bento Grid Layouts**: Used for dashboards and complex information sets.
- **Fluid Responsiveness**: Desktop-first precision with mobile-first code.

### Interaction & Motion
- **Spring Physics**: Use `spring` transitions for a natural, high-end feel.
- **Staggered Entrances**: Elements should enter the screen with a slight delay to create rhythm.
- **Micro-interactions**: Subtle hover states (opacity changes, scale-ups) to provide tactile feedback.

---

## 4. Voice & Tone
- **Sophisticated but Accessible**: We are experts in our field, but we speak to our customers as partners in their design journey.
- **Story-driven**: Every product has a story. We emphasize the artisan, the material, and the heritage.
- **Confident**: We are proud of Nigerian craftsmanship and we don't shy away from its value.

---

## 5. Imagery Guidelines
- **Product Photography**: High-contrast, natural lighting, emphasizing texture and material.
- **Lifestyle**: Minimalist settings that allow the furniture to be the hero.
- **Artisan Focus**: Authentic, behind-the-scenes shots of the making process.
